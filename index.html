Hi, I'm Xin Wang, a Ph.D. student of FVL Lab in the School of Computer Science at Fudan University, advised by Prof. Xingjun Ma and Prof. Yu-Gang Jiang. Prior to this, I worked as a computer vision algorithm engineer at iFLYTEK.

Recently, my research interest lies in the intersection of machine learning safety, security, and alignment. Feel free to reach me at xinwang22 [at] m [dot] fudan [dot] edu [dot] cn, or take my CV.
