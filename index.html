Biography

Currently, I am an Computer Vision Algorithm Engineer at iFlytek. Prior to that, I got my M.Eng. degree from the Faculty of Artificial Intelligence in Education, Central China Normal University (CCNU) in 2021, under the supervision of Prof. Qiusha Min. Previously, I received my B.Eng. degree from the Zhang Zhongjing College of Chinese Medicine, Nanyang Institute of Technology (NYIST) in 2018. During my B.Eng. study, I was an exchange student at the Providence University (PU) in 2016, where I worked with Prof. Cheng-Hsin Wang. Please check my CV for more details.


My research interest lies at the intersection of machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, medical image segmentation, and medical image compression.
